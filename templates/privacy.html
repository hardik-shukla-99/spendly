Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

Spendly ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information when you use our expense tracking service.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the product.
  • Technical data — browser type, device type, and IP address collected automatically by our servers.

We do not collect payment card details, government ID, or any sensitive financial account credentials.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account session.
  • To store and display your expense records.
  • To generate summaries and category breakdowns within the app.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve performance.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply industry-standard measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and access controls to protect your information. While no system is perfectly secure, we take reasonable steps to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

Your expense data is stored for as long as your account remains active. You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove your data from our systems.

5. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — used to load typefaces for the interface. Google may log font requests; see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Hosting provider — our infrastructure provider has access to server logs as part of normal operations.

We do not embed advertising trackers, analytics SDKs, or social media widgets.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Export your expense records at any time from within the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted means you accept the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided within the app.
